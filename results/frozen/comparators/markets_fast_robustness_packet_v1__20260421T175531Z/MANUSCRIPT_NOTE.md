# Manuscript-facing robustness note

## Results-ready sentence
Under the prespecified equal-false-positive band `[0.03, 0.07]`, no tested
fast-family comparator configuration was accepted at 60-, 120-, or 180-minute
segmentation. Under widened post-processing bands that included an upper cutoff
of `0.08`, acceptance appeared only for the 60-minute AC1 configuration,
whereas the canonical 120-minute and 180-minute segmentations remained
non-accepted.

## Short interpretation
The markets fast-family non-acceptance result is robust across tested
segmentations at the locked canonical band, but not fully invariant to post hoc
widening of the acceptable false-positive interval.
